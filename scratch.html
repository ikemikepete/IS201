How to Become A Renaissance Man

You may be asking yourself, "What is a renaissance man?" Dictionary.com's definition is the following:

noun. a cultured man of the Renaissance who was knowledgeable, educated, or proficient in a wide range of fields. (sometimes lowercase) a present-day man who has acquired profound knowledge or proficiency in more than one field.

Why would you want to be a renaissance man? Being a renaissance man entails gaining knowledge, and knowledge is power. By becoming knowledgable in many different areas you will be better equipped to handle the challenges of day to day life. Additionally, you will be able to engage in meaningful discourse with people from all walks of life. People like people they can talk to about the things they value, and as a renaissance man you can be that kind of person for a large swath of the population. This ability to talk with people in a meaningful way will cause people to look up to you, and who wouldn't want to be held in high esteem by their peers? Being a renaissance man doesn't just bring friends and status however, it also enables you to solve problems for others as you use your knowledge to benefit those you come in contact with. After all, with great power comes great responsibility.
