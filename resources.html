Resources

These books have been broken up by category for your convenience. The categories won't cover every field out there, but this is at least a good start. Happy reading!

  1. Philosophy
    • Crime and Punishment by Fyodor Dostoevsky
    • Meditations by Marcus Aurelius
    • Critique of Pure Reason by Immanuel Kant
  2. History
    • The History of the Ancient World: From the Earliest Accounts to the Fall of Rome by Susan Wise Bauer
    • Guns, Germs, and Steel by Jared Diamond
    • Manhunt: The 12-Day Chase for Lincoln's Killer
    • Bomb: The Race to Build—and Steal—the World's Most Dangerous Weapon by Steve Sheinkin
  3. Hard Sciences
    • On the Origin of Species by Charles Darwin
    • A Brief History of Time by Stephen Hawking
    • Cosmos by Carl Sagan
  4. Finances
    • Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki
    • The Total Money Makeover by Dave Ramsey
    • The Millionaire Next Door by Thomas Stanley and William Danko
  5. Economics
    • Free to Choose by Milton and Rose Friedman
    • The Road to Serfdom by Friedrich Hayek
    • The Wealth of Nations by Adam Smith
  6. Politics
    • The Right Side of History by Ben Shapiro
    • The Plot to Change America by Mike Gonzalez
    • Black Rednecks and White Liberals by Thomas Sowell
    • The Declaration of Independence
    • The Constitution of the United States
  7. Self-Improvement
    • The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People by Stephen R Covey
    • 12 Rules for Life: An Antidote to Chaos by Jordan Peterson
    • Willpower Doesn't Work by Benjamin Hardy
  8. Religion
    • The Standard Works: The Bible, The Book of Mormon, The Doctrine and Covenants, The Pearl of Great Price
    • The Apocrypha
    • The Quran
    • A Stranger in Jerusalem: Seeing Jesus as a Jew by Trevan Hatch
    • Mere Christianity by C.S. Lewis
    • The Screwtape Letters by C.S. Lewis
  9. Fiction
    • The Count of Monte Cristo by Alexandre Dumas
    • The Way of Kings by Brandon Sanderson
    • The Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkein
